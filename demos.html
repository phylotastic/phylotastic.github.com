• Phylotastic portal

    View a tree for a list of species (with images and link-outs). The portal will help you make a list by harvesting names from a web page or file (PDF, docx, etc), reading your pre-made list (one name per line or DwC-A) or choosing from a taxon you name (e.g., "Felidae").

  • Phylotastic "trees to go" mobile app

    Snap photos of signs in a zoo, museum, arboretum, etc., and you can use our app to extract out species names, add them to a list, and get make a tree. Available for iOS (go to app store) or Android (ask).

  • DateLife (web form or screencast)

    Wouldn't it be great to calibrate your tree using dates of evolutionary divergence estimated by experts, based on fossil evidence (but without the restrictions of TimeTree)? Welcome to the live demo of DateLife!
